Dots and Boxes

  • High Concept: The player will connect the dots of the same color in as long of lines as possible in order to get the highest score they can in 20 moves. Can you beat my high score of 400???
  • Theme: Iteration Sequence
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Mouse Randomness: Deterministic Genre: Puzzle Play Style: Strategist
  • Player Experience Goals: Players will have to think ahead in order to get the biggest groupings or potentially see an opportunity to create a loop to clear the board of that color.
  • Gameplay: 1. Each turn, the player will draw a line from however many adjacent dots of the same color they want.
    2. The score gets added however many dots the player connected.
    3. If the player was able to connect the dots in such a way that it can form a loop, or it just connects back to itself, every dot of the color is removed from the board.
    4. When a dot is removed, all dots above it will shift down and a new random color dot will appear now on the top to maintain the original square layout.
  • Prototype Goal: To make a working digital game reflecting my analog prototype.
  • Features: This game allows you to acheive a high score and attempt to beat it. One extra twist on this game is that if you make a line connect back to itself when connecting the dots it will clear the entire board of that color. This will allow players opportunities to narrow down the colors on the board in order to obtain a ton of points.
